Information about Destination Darling

About Us

Hello! We're the developers behind Destination Darling: Abby, Ingrid, and Jackie.
Destination Darling arose from a group project in the University of Montana's Web Application Development class. With a shared love for traveling (and planning ahead), creating a social media inspired destination and attractions app only made sense. While still imperfect, Destination Darling provides a place for jet-setters and home-body dreamers alike to share experiences to make planning trips a little easier on everyone.

Application Functionality

At this time, Destination Darling allows a myriad of functionalities with more to be added in the future.
To explore the site, first create your account with us by providing a username, email, and your country of residence. Both email and country of residence are private and will never be publically shared.
Upon account creation, you may notice the status "egg" next to your username on the homepage. This is the basic status all users begin with, and future functionality seeks to implement ranked statuses (hatchling, nestling, fledgling, etc) based on user contributions to the site.
Once registered, you are free to explore the page of destinations, add new destinations, and edit or delete destinations you have created.
Within each destination, you also have access to various user-added attractions (think restaurants, activities, events) at that destination. You can also add new attractions that you think other users would like!

Future Additions

Moving forward, we are looking to add additional functionalities like ranked user statuses, sorting destinations and attractions by various criteria, and external collaborations to allow users to book trips directly from Destination Darling's site. Keep a lookout for these improvements!
